Search

With search, you can easily find projects that match a particular interest and skill.

Messaging

You can easily message a project poster.

You can also follow up on posters with its intuitive inbox and chat interface.

Notification

Each time a message is sent to you, you get an email notification.

You also get emails containing links to projects posted that match your interests and skills.

You also get emails containing links to the profiles of new users with similar skills and interests.
