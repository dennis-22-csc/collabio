Messaging

The messaging feature enables you to easily message a project poster.

Its intuitive inbox and chat interface also enables you to further the conversation with them.

Watch this demo to see this in action.

Notification

The notification feature ensures you get an email notification each time a new message is sent.

You also get emails containing links to projects posted that match your interests and skills.

You also get emails containing links to the profiles of new users with similar skills and interests.
