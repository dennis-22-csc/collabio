Collabio Features

Project

This is the core of the Collabio platform. Watch this demo to see how you can easily get started posting a project.

Search

With search, you can easily find projects that match a particular interest and skill. Here is a demo.

Messaging

You can easily message a project poster. You can also follow up on posters with its intuitive inbox and chat interface. Watch this demo to see this in action.

Notification

Each time a message is sent to you, you get an email notification.

At the end of the week, you get an email notification containing links to projects posted during the week that match your interests and skills.

Also at the end of the week, you get an email notification containing links to new users with similar skills and interests.
